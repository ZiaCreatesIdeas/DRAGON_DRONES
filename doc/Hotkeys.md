# Hotkeys

* Key:  
[X, Y, Z] means choose 'X' or 'Y' or 'Z'.  
The '+' sign means to press and hold.   
LMB = Left Mouse Button.   
RMB = Right Mouse Button.  
MMB = Middle Mouse Button.
