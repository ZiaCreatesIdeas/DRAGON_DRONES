# Hotkeys

```{note}
:class: warning
[X, Y, Z] means choose 'X' or 'Y' or 'Z' on the keyboard.  
The '+' sign implies a combination of mouse clicks and button presses.   
LMB =  Left Mouse Button.   
RMB =  Right Mouse Button.  
MMB =  Middle Mouse Button. 
```
